SAMGYUPSAL

Directly translated from Korean, samgyeop-sal (삼겹살) means "three layer flesh," referring to striations of lean meat and fat in the pork belly that appear as three layers when cut

ADOBO

It is a popular Filipino dish and cooking process in Philippine cuisine that involves meat, seafood, or vegetables marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns, which is browned in oil, and simmered in the marinade.

INIHAW NA BANGUS

Inihaw na Bangus means Grilled Milkfish. This is a very tangy and delicious dish that anyone can prepare easily. It goes well with a soy sauce dip and rice.
